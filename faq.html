FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions about travel and booking

TOP SELLERS

Find answers to the your questions about our travel packages, booking process, payment options, and travel policies.

How do I book a tour package?

You can book a tour package by going to the Packages page, selecting a package, and completing the form with your details and payment.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Yes, you can cancel or modify your booking from the booking dashboard within 7-days of booking. Cancellation charges may apply.

Are flights included in the tour packages?

Flights are not included unless explicitly mentioned in the package details or you contact us and we would book a flight for you at lowest price possible.

What documents do I need for international travel?

You need a valid passport, visa (if applicable), and any country-specific travel documents. Kindly visit your respective embassy to avoid later issues.

Is travel insurance included?

Travel insurance is optional. You can add it during checkout for extra safety and coverage. Once added you can't remove it later.

Still Have Questions?

Our travel experts are here to help! If you couldn't find the answer you were looking for, don't hesitate to reach out to us directly.
